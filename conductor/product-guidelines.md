# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Professional, friendly, and clear. Avoid jargon where possible.
- **Clarity:** Use simple, direct language. Keep error messages actionable and descriptive.
- **Formatting:** Use sentence case for headings and titles.

## Branding
- **Color Palette:** Modern, clean, and accessible. Primary actions should be clearly distinguished using a consistent brand color.
- **Typography:** Highly legible sans-serif fonts optimized for both mobile and desktop screens.

## UX Principles
- **Simplicity:** Minimize the number of clicks required to add an expense or settle a debt.
- **Transparency:** Clearly display how balances are calculated to avoid confusion among group members.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast, keyboard navigability, and screen-reader support throughout the application.
